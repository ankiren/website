# US-7.2: Background Music

## Description

As a **user**,
I want to **have background music that plays during my focus sessions**,
So that **I can maintain concentration and enjoy a pleasant study environment**.

---

## Acceptance Criteria

- AC-7.2.1: Music automatically starts when timer begins
- AC-7.2.2: User can toggle music on/off manually
- AC-7.2.3: Different music track for each session type (Focus, Short Break, Long Break)
- AC-7.2.4: Music loops continuously during active session
- AC-7.2.5: Music pauses when timer pauses
